Acro is building the future

Acro envisions the future of crypto infrastructure, anticipating its trajectory over the next 5 to 10
years. By recognizing the infrastructure of the new financial system can be split into three
different technology battles:

1. The Currency/Settlement Layer battle
2. The Virtual Machine battle
3. The Scalability battle

Acro determined the victors in these pivotal technological arenas to be a Layer 2 EVM on top of
Bitcoin.

The Layer 1 Battle

Acro harnesses the power of Bitcoin as the bedrock of true decentralization.
Unrivaled in security and trust, Bitcoin's status as the most decentralized asset fortifies our commitment to building on the ultimate settlement layer.

Virtual Machine battle

Capitalizing on Ethereum's thriving ecosystem and dominant lead in applications and developers, the Ethereum Virtual Machine reigns as the premier virtual machine. Just as Windows prevailed in the '80s, momentum drives the victor!

Scaleability battle

Acro embraces a layered strategy , eclipsing bridge solutions with unmatched security. By directly inheriting Bitcoin's robust protection, Acro fortifies your assets with the pinnacle of crypto defense.

We're building an EVM on top of Bitcoin


Acro unites Bitcoin's market cap with Ethereum's dApp ecosystem, fusing the best of both worlds. Bitcoin's fair distribution, decentralization, and unparalleled security meet Ethereum's diverse applications, igniting a new frontier.
